Question 1, Model 1 Analysis: Although we observe statistically significant results for our variables (albeit at varying levels of significance), this model does not help us fully identify how COVID has impacted the retail industry. Our residual plotting chart shows that a LOGIT model may work better as we are using Unemployed (our binary dependent variable) as the ultimate variable of interest. Based on this analysis, we wanted to introduce an interaction term for question one in order to identify the impact of COVID after April 1, 2020, the date that we assume COVID would have the biggest impact on Unemployment which is shown in our next model.
